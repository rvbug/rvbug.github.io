Privacy Policy

Last updated: 16 Sept 2025

Philosophy

Your privacy matters. QubitAI.in is built with a privacy-first approach. I believe in transparency and minimal data collection.

What I DON'T Collect

  • No analytics or tracking of any kind
  • No personal information (names, emails, addresses)
  • No cookies for tracking or functionality
  • No contact forms or data submission
  • No user accounts or registration data
  • No behavioral tracking across pages
  • No third-party advertising networks
  • No social media pixels or trackers

What I DO Collect

Absolutely nothing. This website operates without collecting any personal data whatsoever.

Technical Details

  • No server-side logging of personal information
  • No JavaScript tracking scripts
  • Static website with no data collection capabilities
  • SSL/TLS encryption for secure connections
  • No cookies placed on your device

External Links

This website contains links to external services like:

  • GitHub repositories (subject to GitHub's privacy policy)
  • Social media profiles (subject to their respective privacy policies)
  • Third-party tools and resources (each has their own privacy policies)

When you click these links, you leave this site and are subject to those platforms' privacy practices.

Open Source Projects

  • Code repositories are publicly available on GitHub
  • Project documentation is provided for educational purposes
  • No usage tracking of downloads or repository views from our end

Your Rights

  • Browse anonymously by default
  • No opt-out needed since we don't collect data
  • Complete transparency about our practices
  • Contact us if you have privacy questions

Changes to This Policy

Since I collect no data, changes to this policy would only occur if we ever decide to implement tracking (which we have no plans to do). Any such changes would be clearly announced.

Contact

Questions about this privacy policy? Reach out via the contact methods on the site.
